Toasting Bread for Beginners

Now, I know what you're thinking. I thought this was a l33t c0d!ng tutorial site. Well, turns out I'm hungry, so before we move to Serious h4ck!ng, let's learn how to make food. After all, a nourished hacker is an effective hacker.

Requirements:

  • A toaster
  • A thorough understanding of thermodynamics and physical chemistry
  • A (piece of) bread
  • An electrical outlet

Instructions:

  1. Plug the toaster into the outlet. Preferably don't do this with wet hands.
  2. Take a piece of bread/toast and sing to it a song of your choice softly.
  3. Stick it into the toaster until the toaster yells at you that the bread is ready
  4. Cover it with peanut butter and jelly and nom it down.
