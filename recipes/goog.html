How to open a Google Chrome page

This is a simple informative tutorial on how to open a Google Chrome page. You're welcome. (How are you even reading this?)

Requirements:

  • Computer
  • Internet connection
  • Google Chrome
  • Mouse, trackpad
  • (Optional) A Bible

Instructions:

  1. Open your computer. (Seriously, how are you even reading this?)
  2. Open your terminal. (If you don't know what that is, goo- oh wait.)
  3. Type in "$open -a "Google Chrome.app"".
  4. Tah-dah!!!
